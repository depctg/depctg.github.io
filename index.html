Publications Experience

I’m Zhiyuan Guo. I’m currently a PhD student at the University of California, San Diego, major in Computer Science. I am now a part of the Wuklab and SysNet group in UCSD, and luckily advised by Yiying Zhang. I’m interesting in and working on research ideas that enables resource disaggregation in datacenter, from designing new disaggregated hardware to building operating system, runtime and programming language support. I got my bachelor degree from Beihang University, Beijing in 2019.
